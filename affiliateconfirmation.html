How to create a QR code for your affiliate link

To create a QR code for your affiliate link using Google Chrome, follow these steps:

  1. After receiving your unique link, copy it.
  2. Open a new tab in Chrome and paste your link.
  3. Click on the address bar and look for the QR code icon on the right side.
  4. Click the QR code icon and follow the prompts to download the QR code.
